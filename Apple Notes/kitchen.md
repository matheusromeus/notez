Minimum utensils for basic cooking and meal prep:

**Cooking / Heating**
	•	1 medium saucepan (2–3 L) – for rice, lentils, boiling
	•	1 frying pan / skillet (non-stick preferred) – for eggs, sautéing vegetables, meat
	•	1 small pot – for sauces, small portions

**Cutting / Prep**
	•	1 chef’s knife – general chopping
	•	1 paring knife – small tasks, peeling
	•	1 cutting board – preferably plastic or wood

**Measuring / Mixing**
	•	1 set measuring cups
	•	1 set measuring spoons
	•	1 mixing bowl (medium)

**Eating / Serving**
	•	1 spatula – for frying pan
	•	1 ladle – for soups, dal, rice
	•	1 serving spoon

**Storage**
	•	3–4 airtight containers – for meal prep and leftovers

Optional but useful:
	•	Peeler
	•	Tongs
	•	Whisk
